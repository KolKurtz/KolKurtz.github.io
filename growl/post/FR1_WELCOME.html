NEW CONCEPT ART

The Primacy in-system map is now real-time. This allows some far more interesting mechanics as far as dodging contact that you don't want. It also takes a bit of pressure off of the otherwise turn based nature of the game. A bit of variety is always welcome.


This is a pretty late design decision I know. Thankfully the old turn based approach was very close to simulating real time anyway so the only thing required was an animation loop and handling of keyboard input.

The most interesting new question is going to be how control of real-time will work on tablets? Perhaps a virtual on-screen gamepad....
