THE ASCENDANCY OF A SPECIES

A PIXELART GRAND STRATEGY SIM

Explore, fight, develop, trade.

EXPLORE A GALAXY OF POSSIBILITIES

Features

  • Procedurally generated events and solar systems
  • Multiple gameplay modes including dice and turn-based strategy
  • Snarky AIs
  • Five playable races
  • Massive explorable galaxy
  • Complex interaction and inter-species politics
  • Problems with animal hair
  • Planetary colonization and development
  • Un-ending space warfare

Gameplay

A vast galaxy awaits you. Unknown perils lurk in unexplored sectors. Careful diplomacy will grant you new opportunities with competing species.

Seize advantage for your own species where you can in the race to inherit the gifts of the ancients. Only one race will reach primacy.

Play your own way. All activites generate experience and experience is the key!

PLAY IN YOUR BROWSER

Primacy is designed to work in any modern browser supporting a display width of 1280 pixels or wider. Touch controls are supported as are gamepads!

Create a login to establish your species and benefit from cloud saves. Play your adventure from anywhere the internet is accessible.
